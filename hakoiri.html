インスタレーション。音声は、私が私の作品について延々２時間語ったものを編集して流してます 。
i graduated Japanese art univ. there is no teacher who taught me how to explain my work or myself, but i recognized this is the very important thing as i live and work as an artist. i told about me and my work in TV in this installation. the title "HAKOIRIMUSUME"Japanese traditional slang means a girl who is treated precious like boxed off from various "bad" things by her parents. (HAKO means box) i think Japanese art univ. almost have nothing to do with other social world. this situation looks like HAKOIRIMUSUME.
